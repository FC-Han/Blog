On this page

Plastic gear loss estimates from remote observation of industrial fishing activity ​

通过估计渔具损耗来间接计算在海中丢弃的渔具残骸。

数据：

  • AIS的捕捞时间数据
  • 渔船长度、功率、吨位
  • Fishery landings

采用Unit gear model

Gear intensity and dissipation were modelled according to the framework described in Kuczenski.

对于给定的fishery f，我们定义gear P每年丢失到海洋中的质量为：

Pf=Ff⋅uf⋅gf⋅df

其中：

  • F：total capture of fish by the fishery, including landed catch and discards
  • u：effort intensity of the fishery(effort per unit catch)
  • g: gear intensity of the effort (mass of gear per scaling paramerter)
  • d: rate at which gear is dissipated into the ocean, having units of fractional mass dissipated per operating time.
