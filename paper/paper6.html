Multispecies population-scale emergence of climate change signals in an ocean warming hotspot ​

主要研究的环境变化为：

  • ocean warming (the long-term trend)
  • marine heatwaves (shorter-term periods above long-term average temperatures)

三个方面来研究鱼类种群：

  • spatial distribution
  • individual growth
  • population productivity

方法 ​

根据预先设定好的准则选择了49种物种，计算每种物种的5个生物量指标：

  1. center of latitude
  2. center of longitude
  3. depth
  4. sea surface temperature
  5. bottom temperature

计算公式为：

Xj=∑i=1nwiXijSspring ∑wi+∑i=1nwiXij Fall ∑wi2

其中X为参数(经度、纬度、深度...)，而j为年份，wi为物种i的丰度。

Differences in distribution (latitude, longitude, depth, surface temperature, and bottom temperature) and size-at-age (length and weight) metrics were compared for each species during the decades spanning 1970–2009 and the recent decade of 2010–2019.

对于其Population productivity data，我们计算每个种群的spawning stock biomass (SSB) and recruitment (R) 的比率，即 R/SSB。数据从NOAA的报告中获取。
